DSP Correctness Notes
Fs = 700 Hz. Nyquist = 350 Hz. All noise sources are well below this. The 40 Hz lowpass attenuates EMG before the notch removes the residual mains line.
Pad = 2100 smp = 1.5 × (Fs / fc) = 1.5 × 1400. Reflect-padding uses signal[PAD−1−i] (off-by-one corrected) to mirror both edges symmetrically, minimising the DC step that would otherwise ring through the 0.5 Hz highpass.
Notch centre frequency updates in real-time to match the hum slider (45–65 Hz range covers both EU 50 Hz and US 60 Hz mains). EMG is modelled as white noise fed through a 1st-order highpass at the onset frequency, then RMS-normalised.
